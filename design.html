Screen Printing

Small to Large format presses to offer the best efficiencies in producing your product. State of the art high-speed printing presses with many in-line capabilities for producing tighter registration of colors with less labor to offer attractive pricing.


Our expertise in art and color development coupled with our knowledgeable press operators allows BGS to produce the smoothest quality halftone fades with reduced graininess of dots. Screen printing utilizing UV inks to provide the most durable product for outdoor uses.

Digital Printing

The era of short run, fast turnaround, production graphics is upon us. BGS provides cutting edge high resolution solvent-based digital graphics on the latest substrates for digital printing. Digital printing allows you the flexibility to print small quantities without the normal setup cost associated with longer runs thereby saving you money. Copy can be updated and changed frequently with short runs to reduce the cost of using or throwing out obsolete printed products. This press is great for short - medium runs of pressure sensitive labels, short - medium runs of custom graphics, signage, and even prototypes.


BGS provides cutting-edge high-resolution solvent-based inks with UV coated graphics on the latest flexible and rigid substrates for digital printing. Pre-production preparation is quick allowing for fast turn-around and lower cost on smaller volume orders. Photographic quality with smooth halftone production. Can be provided for 3-5 year outdoor durability.
